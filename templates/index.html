About Us

Ransomware is a type of cryptovirological malware that permanently block access to the victim's personal data unless a ransom is paid. While some simple ransomware may lock the system without damaging any files, more advanced malware uses a technique called cryptoviral extortion.

Protect from Ransomware

Keep offline data backups stored in locations that are air-gapped or inaccessible from any potentially infected computer, such as disconnected external storage drives or the cloud, which prevents the ransomware from accessing them.

Power down devices if you are unable to disconnect them from the network to avoid further spread of the ransomware infection. Note: This step will prevent your organization from maintaining ransomware infection artifacts and potential evidence stored in volatile memory.
